Who We Are

Afrilink Agency is a licensed recruitment agency based in Kenya. Our mission is to connect skilled and semi-skilled workers with reputable employers across the globe. We specialize in secure, transparent, and legal overseas job placements.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted gateway for Kenyan talent seeking international career opportunities.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Honest and transparent processes.
  • Safety: Verified employers and secure travel.
  • Empowerment: Helping Kenyans achieve their dreams.
