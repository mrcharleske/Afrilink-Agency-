About Afrilink Agency

About Us

Founded in 2020 in Nairobi, Afrilink Agency emerged during a time of global uncertainty to connect Kenyan talent with life-changing job opportunities abroad. Starting with a small team passionate about empowering youth, we’ve grown to place over 500 individuals in roles across Canada, UK, Dubai, Qatar, and Saudi Arabia. Our personalized approach ensures every client receives tailored support, from resume crafting to visa processing, transforming lives and communities.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to empowering Kenyans by providing ethical, transparent job placement services. Our mission is to break down barriers—be it visas, skills, or connections—ensuring every client finds a safe, rewarding job abroad that supports their dreams and families back home.

Our Vision

We envision a future where Afrilink Agency transforms the lives of 1,000+ Kenyans by 2030, expanding our reach to new countries and industries. We aim to be the leading agency for international job placements, known for integrity, excellence, and life-changing impact.
